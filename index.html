The Story Behind the MVP

How we discovered the path to intelligent field operations

The Challenge

2020 Companies manages thousands of field representatives across major retail programs. The complexity of scheduling, training, and reporting was becoming unsustainable.

Key Insight: 70% of management time was spent on repetitive tasks that could be automated.

The Discovery

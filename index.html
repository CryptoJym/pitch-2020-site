The Story Behind the MVP

How we discovered the path to intelligent field operations

01

The Challenge

2020 Companies manages thousands of field representatives across major retail programs. The complexity of scheduling, training, and reporting was becoming unsustainable.

Key Insight: 70% of management time was spent on repetitive tasks that could be automated.
02

The Discovery

Through 16 passes of analysis, we identified the core operational rail: Assignment ‚Üí Evidence ‚Üí Exception ‚Üí Approval ‚Üí Report.

Key Insight: This pattern repeated across all programs, making it perfect for AI automation.
03

The Solution

An AI-powered system that could handle 80% of routine operations, freeing managers to focus on strategy and relationships.

Key Insight: Start with Samsung's structured program as the pilot, then expand.

AI Integration Points

  • Assignment Intelligence: ML-based scheduling optimization
  • Evidence Processing: Computer vision for retail compliance
  • Exception Handling: NLP for automated escalation
  • Reporting: Real-time analytics and insights

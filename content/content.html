Replace vs Augment vs No‑Go

Replace

  • Manual photo QA reviewers
  • Report assemblers (daily/weekly/QBR)
  • Dispatch/scheduling coordinators
  • Recruiting schedulers/coordinators
  • Install proof‑pack clerks
  • Routine content drafters

Augment

  • Reps/Brand Ambassadors/Installers (micro‑training, dynamic checklists)
  • Field Managers (triage/approve/coach)
  • Program/Regional Ops (QBRs/forecasts)
  • Recruiters (fit/offer)
  • Sales/Client Success; Finance/Legal/HRBPs

No‑Go

  • Hiring/firing & discipline
  • Legal/compliance determinations; contract terms
  • Sensitive state benefits comms (where unsettled)

Change Management

  • Manager huddles: 15–30 min weekly during pilot.
  • Screen captures: 2–3 min per workflow; SharePoint-hosted.
  • Support lane: dedicated Slack/Teams with SLAs.
  • Audit lanes: random sample + override UX.
  • Incentives check: ensure quotas/KPIs align with exception surfacing.

Confirm‑Live Checklist

  1. Contracting entity (MSAs) and data‑rights confirmation (de‑identified photos/visit logs) — brand exceptions noted.
  2. Field app export/webhooks; Workday API creds; BI workspace.
  3. Exception taxonomy; report templates; pilot markets (8–12 across 2–3 retailers).
  4. Brand allow/deny list for AI training and reporting imagery.
